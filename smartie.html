Snorting Smarties Will Kill You Instantly, Experts Scream
By Trisha Calamity | April 8, 2025

If you or someone you know has even sniffed a Smarties wrapper today, you may be entitled to a funeral. In an alarming rise of absolute chaos, teens have started snorting Smarties — and according to reports that may or may not exist, this results in guaranteed instant death.

“Just one roll is all it takes,” said Dr. Morty Dreadwell, a guy in a lab coat we found pacing outside a gas station. “One roll is actually more lethal than snorting plutonium dust off a cactus in space.”

Experts believe the Smartie particles travel directly to the brain and explode like tiny sugary bombs. Symptoms include: becoming extremely cool for 2 seconds, sneezing chalk, and spontaneous disappearance from the mortal realm.

The CDC (Cool Dude Committee) has issued a Level 9000 Warning: “Do not snort Smarties. Ever. Not even one. ESPECIALLY not one. One roll is a one-way ticket to the grave.”

Globul Articles reached out to the Smarties company for comment. They screamed and hung up.
